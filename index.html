Qwixx

A fast-paced dice rolling game

Instructions

  • Objective: Score as many points as possible by crossing out numbers on the board from left to right.
  • The more numbers of each color that are crossed out, the higher they are worth.
  • To start your turn, roll the dice and add them together to equal the number you would like to cross out for a specific color.
  • During your turn, you may either add together the white dice and cross out any color with that total or you can add together one of the white dice and one of the colored dice to cross out a number of that color.
  • If you would like to, you can cross out both a white combination and a combination of a white die and a colored die but, you must do the white combination first.
  • Numbers must be crossed out from left to right. Once you cross out a number, you cannot cross a number to the left of it.
  • If you cannot or do not cross out at least one combination, you must take a penalty (in the bottom right of the board) for -5 points each.
  • If you have at least 5 numbers crossed out of a color, you are able to "lock out" that color by crossing out the "12", "2" or "L" for that color. "Locking out" a color will cross off both the "12" and "L" and count towards you total points. It also removes that color from the game.
  • The game ends when two colors have been "locked out" or when all four penalties have been marked.
